What is Physical Therapy?

A form of healthcare that helps to relieve pain and improve mobility. It also allows a person to live a more normal life. Physical therapy is most commonly used for recovering from an injury or surgery. However, it can also be used to treat any pain condition or improve balance.

Physical therapists are licensed and trained medical professionals who provide physical therapy. Physical therapists are skilled in treating physical disorders, mobility and maintaining health, as well as helping to promote physical activity. A physical therapist in the United States must be a licensed doctor of physical therapy and have passed a licensure exam.

Overview

Physical impairments of the nervous, musculoskeletal and cardiovascular systems are evaluated by physiotherapists. The goal of treatment is to maximize functional performance and promote independence.

Patient education and active participation in physiotherapy is very important in helping recovery. Physiotherapy treatment is offered in collaboration with other healthcare team members. All members of the Quebec Order of Physiotherapists (OPPQ) are part of JGH's physiotherapy staff.

The Beverly Physiotherapyt provides services via a hybrid model of tele rehabilitation and on-site visits.
